Key Operational Advantages

  • Scalability: A single Tier-2 AI agent can handle 50 concurrent calls, each lasting 11 minutes on average, resulting in 272.5 calls/hour or 2,180 calls per 8-hour shift.
  • 24/7 Operation: AI agents operate continuously without fatigue, absenteeism, or declining performance, ensuring 99.9% uptime.
  • KPI Excellence: AI agents consistently meet key performance indicators (KPIs) at Six Sigma levels (99.9997%), which is nearly impossible for human agents to maintain.
  • Energy Efficiency: Despite 24/7 operation, the energy costs for AI infrastructure amount to only $18,731 over 5 years, with a modest 2% annual increase.
  • Human Resource Optimization: The remaining human agents (5%) can focus on high-value strategic roles such as process oversight and complex case resolution.
